Crochet is divided into the following types: simple knitting with a short hook, Tunisian knitting with a long hook. There are two types of knitting: flat and circular. In simple crochet with flat knitting, the direction of movement forward and backward is possible - with such knitting there is no wrong side, or only forward, with a break and fastening of the thread at the end of each row. In circular knitting, products are either knitted in a cylindrical shape (without a seam) or circular. The main types of loops in crochet: air, half-column, single crochet, double crochet.
